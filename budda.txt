LU MEI CON CHOG SUM GYI NGO VO NYI
The very essence of the infallible Three Jewels,
NGEN SONG JIK LEI DROL WAI PA VO TE
The hero who frees the lower realms from suffering,
DAK PAI SHING DU DRO PEI DE PYOEN CHOG
The supreme helmsman for those who travel to the pure fields:
I pray to the Protector Amitabha!
